## 2. General Information on Data Processing

As a rule, we process personal data only to the extent necessary to provide a functional website and to ensure the stability and security of the website. The legal basis for such processing is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. Where further processing takes place in individual cases, it will only be carried out on the basis of a legal permission or your consent. Art. 13 GDPR requires transparency regarding the purposes of processing, legal bases, recipients, and any transfers to third countries.

## 3. Provision of the Website and Hosting via GitHub Pages

This website is hosted via **GitHub Pages**, a service provided by **GitHub, Inc.** GitHub states that when a GitHub Pages website is visited, the visitor’s IP address is logged and stored for security purposes, regardless of whether the visitor is signed in to GitHub or not.

In this context, the following technical data may in particular be processed:

- IP address
- date and time of the request
- requested file or URL
- amount of data transferred
- notification of successful retrieval
- browser type and browser version
- operating system
- referrer URL

The processing is carried out for the purpose of providing the website, ensuring its technical stability, and maintaining the security of the service. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. Our legitimate interest lies in the secure and reliable delivery of the website.

([What is GitHub Pages?](https://docs.github.com/en/pages/getting-started-with-github-pages/what-is-github-pages))  
([GitHub General Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement))

## 4. External Images Hosted by Imgur

Some images on this website are loaded from external servers operated by **Imgur**.

When you visit a page containing such an image, your browser retrieves the image file directly from Imgur’s servers. In this process, technical access data may be transmitted to Imgur, in particular your IP address and browser-related request data. Depending on your browser and configuration, the referrer URL may also be transmitted.

This processing is carried out for the purpose of displaying the embedded images and ensuring the technical delivery of the content. The legal basis is Art. [6(1)(f) GDPR](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2016/679/oj/eng). Our legitimate interest lies in the efficient presentation of the website and its visual content.

([Imgur Privacy Policy](https://imgur.com/privacy))  
([Imgur Terms of Service](https://imgur.com/tos))

## 5. Site Search via Algolia

This website uses **Algolia** to provide site search functionality.

When you use the search function, your search terms and technical request data may be processed by **Algolia** in order to provide search results and maintain the search service. The legal basis for the use of the search function is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. Our legitimate interest lies in providing an efficient and user-friendly search function for the content of this website.

([Algolia Privacy Policy](https://www.algolia.com/policies/privacy))

## 6. Recipients of the Data

Recipients of personal data processed in connection with the use of this website may include:

- **GitHub**, whose infrastructure is used to host and deliver this website via GitHub Pages
- **Imgur**, where individual image files are loaded from external servers

To the extent these providers process personal data, this takes place in connection with the technical operation, delivery, security, and search functionality of the website or its externally loaded content. GitHub provides further information in its General Privacy Statement, and Imgur and Algolia provide privacy-related information in their own privacy resources.

GitHub states that personal data may be processed in the United States and other countries where GitHub, its affiliates, subsidiaries, or subprocessors operate. For international transfers, GitHub states that it generally relies on the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses and also complies with the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework, the UK Extension to the EU-U.S. DPF, and the Swiss-U.S. Data Privacy Framework.

## 9. Contact

If you contact us by email, the personal data you provide will be processed solely for the purpose of handling your inquiry. The legal basis for this processing is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. If your inquiry is aimed at concluding or performing a contract, the additional legal basis is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR. Our legitimate interest lies in the proper handling of your inquiry.

## 10. Storage Period

We do not store personal data ourselves unless this is necessary in individual cases, for example when you contact us by email.

Where personal data is processed by third-party providers such as GitHub, Imgur, or Algolia, the storage period is determined by the respective provider in accordance with its own privacy policy and applicable legal requirements. GitHub states that it retains personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the relevant purposes, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.
